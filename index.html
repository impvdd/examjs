About

Welcome to our unique universe of characters from the animated series "Rick and Morty"! We are a team of enthusiasts who give you the opportunity to acquire your own character from this exciting and crazy world.

Who are we ?

We are a team of Rick and Morty fans united by a common passion for this amazing animated series. Our characters are not just figurines or images, they are a piece of this amazing world that you can bring to your home.

What do we offer ?

We offer unique and high-quality characters from "Rick and Morty", created with a love of detail. Whether you are a long-awaited collector or just a fan, we have something special for everyone.

Why choose us ?

  • Originality: Each of our characters is unique and made with special attention to detail.
  • Quality: We are proud to offer high-quality products that delight the eye and embody the spirit of the animated series.
  • Love of things: We don't just sell products, we do it with love and passion for the world of "Rick and Morty", and we hope that they bring you joy as well as us.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the process of selecting and ordering a character on your website?

On our website, the choice of a character is very simple. You just go to the "Characters" page and view the available characters from the animated series "Rick and Morty". As soon as you select the desired hero, click the "Add to cart" button and follow the instructions to place an order.

Can I get information about the characteristics or features of each character before buying?

Of course! On the page of each character, we provide detailed information about his characteristics, history and interesting facts from the animated series. This will help you choose the character that best suits your preferences.

What payment options do you offer to purchase characters from the animated series?

We offer a variety of payment options, including credit and debit cards, electronic payments, as well as payments through electronic money platforms such as PayPal or WebMoney.

Is it possible to place an order for a character with individual or personalized details?

Unfortunately, at the moment we do not provide services for customizing characters. However, we try to offer a wide selection of standard characters from the animated series.

What are the terms of delivery and return of products if they do not meet expectations or have defects?

We guarantee the quality of our products. If the product arrived with defects or does not meet your expectations, please contact us within 14 days after receiving the order, and we will find the best solution - replacement of the product or refund. Delivery is carried out by reliable services so that your order arrives safely and on time.
